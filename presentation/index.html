Special Relativity in Virtual Reality

Thomas Longo, Vince Mutolo, David Stilwell

Initial Goals

Create Basic Physics Engine

The engine needed to at least handle:

  • Central force motion
    • Double pendulum
    • N-body problem
  • Relativistic physics
    • Contraction in 3 dimensions
    • Contraction based on relative velocity of reference frame (camera) to moving objects

Methods

Unity was used for:

  • Graphical processing
  • Application of discrete time-step physics
  • Intra-environment controls and objectives
  • Interfacing with cave virtual reality software
